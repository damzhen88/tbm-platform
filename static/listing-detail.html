Business Overview

Siam Agri-Tech Solutions is at the forefront of the digital revolution in Southeast Asian agriculture. Founded in 2018, we have developed a comprehensive ecosystem that connects small-hold farmers directly with international supply chains through AI-driven logistics and real-time yield monitoring.

Our platform currently serves over 15,000 farmers across Thailand, increasing their average profit margins by 22% through reduced waste and optimized pricing models. We are now seeking Series A funding to scale our operations into Vietnam and Indonesia.
